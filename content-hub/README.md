## Platform Distribution Checklist

For each piece of content, distribute to:

- [ ] **LinkedIn** - Professional audience, thought leadership
- [ ] **Twitter/X** - Tech community, viral potential
- [ ] **YouTube** - Long-form video content
- [ ] **TikTok** - Short-form video clips
- [ ] **Instagram** - Carousel posts, reels
- [ ] **Blog** - SEO-optimized articles
- [ ] **Newsletter** - Email subscribers
- [ ] **Medium** - Cross-post articles
